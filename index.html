Vastu-Sastra

Vāstu-Śastras (literally, science of dwelling) are ancient Sanskrit manuals of architecture. These contain Vastu-Vidya (literally, knowledge of dwelling).

Vāstu-Śastras, from our ancient and spiritual culture, delve deeply into science of integrating and living in harmony with nature. They include extensive guidelines and treatises on home design, construction and town planning (kingdoms in ancient times). Its how villages, towns and kingdoms integrated temples, residences, palaces, gardens, water bodies and various elements within them to achieve oneness with mother nature.

Life in harmony

with the elements

It is intrinsic to Vāstu-Śastras that the five elements known as the pancha-maha-bhutas - Prithvi (Earth), Ap (Water), Vayu (Air), Agni (Fire), Akasha (Ether/Space) have to be in balance for well-being. There is an invisible and dynamic relationship in the mix and interplay of the five elements in our lives - a very fine balance. It is believed that by harnessing the benefits bestowed by the balance of these elements, one can lead a harmonious life.

Vayu {Air}

The Air element is gentle, uplifting, nurturing, healing, freeing and balancing.

It represents different types of 'Prānā' energy.

The gentle breeze that rustles through the trees and dances across our terrace embodies the element of air, bringing a sense of lightness and movement to Skanda Nilaya.

Ap {Water}

Water symbolizes the fluidity and adaptability in life.

Flowing through our gardens, homes while nourishing the Skanda Nilaya landscape, the element water governs fluidity, purification, nourishment and controls the movement of energy, fluids and the physical body.

The water element is soothing and sensual, and helps connect us to our feelings and emotions.

Agni {Fire}

The Fire element is hot, bright, dynamic, strengthening and stimulating.

This element governs passion, anger, ambition, desire, willpower, courage, confidence, self-expression, creative thinking, and leadership

At the heart of Skanda Nilaya, the element of fire ignites the spirit of creativity and passion, infusing our spaces with warmth and energy

Akasha {Ether}

Ether - the space that permeates, connects all things and holds infinite potential.

The Ether or space element is neither visible nor audible. It is expansive, calming, intuitive and boundless.

At Skanda Nilaya, amidst the vast expanse of nature, residents can experience a deep sense of mindfulness, allowing the beauty of the present to unfold in all its splendor.

Prithvi {Earth}

Earth is the foundational element of the Panchabhūtas.

This aspect of nature governs groundedness, stability, strength, permanence, patience, fertility, and security.

At Skanda Nilaya, the earth beneath our feet grounds us in the present moment, connecting with the rich tapestry of nature that surrounds us.
